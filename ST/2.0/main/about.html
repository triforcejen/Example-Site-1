About Us

Our core business is the supply and fix of wall and floor tiles to the commercial market, major house builders and domestic properties.

We are located in the South of the UK and have good access to core markets and major transport links.

CBA Tiling and Just Tiling are both industry leading names in Tiling Contracting, established in 1987 and 1988.

Both businesses were purchased in 2010 and combined trading as S.T. Contracting Ltd to form one of the largest tiling contractors in the UK.

S.T. Contracting Ltd. has an excellent reputation and has grown considerably since 2010.

Success based on repeat business with "Key Customers and Suppliers", excellent quality and customer service.
