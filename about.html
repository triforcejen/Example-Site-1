About Us

Our core business is the supply and fix of wall and floor tiles to the commercial market, major house builders and domestic properties.

We are located in the South of the UK and have good access to core markets and major transport links.

ST Contracting acquired CBA Tiling, Just Tiling and Stratford Ceramics industry leading names in Tiling Contracting, established in 1987, 1988 and 2006 respectively.

All businesses were purchased in 2010 and combined trading as ST Contracting Ltd to form one of the largest tiling contractors in the UK.

ST Contracting Ltd. has an excellent reputation and has grown considerably since 2010.

Success based on repeat business with "Key Customers and Suppliers", excellent quality and customer service.
